# Wikipedia AI Governance Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

Our comprehensive analysis of Wikipedia's governance framework has revealed significant AI-related policy development and discussion across key governance pages. We analyzed 42 critical governance pages and found **16 pages (38.1%) contain AI-related content** with a total of **581 AI references**.

## Key Findings

### 1. AI Governance Coverage by Page Type

| Page Type | AI Coverage | Key Insight |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Project/Assessment Pages** | 100% (2/2) | Complete coverage - all pages address AI |
| **Bot/Automation Policy** | 66.7% (2/3) | Strong AI integration in automation governance |
| **Guidelines** | 66.7% (2/3) | Critical guidelines like "Reliable Sources" heavily address AI |
| **Help/Community Pages** | 66.7% (2/3) | Community actively discussing AI usage |
| **Policy** | 33.3% (1/3) | Limited core policy coverage but significant depth where present |
